Sword X Staff Food System

Intro

The food system in SxS is an overlooked, mainly free-to-play in-game system to increase stats during battles and grant permanent stats depending on the type and rarity of the food recipe.

Recipe materials can and should be farmed daily from each of the world maps in game.

There are daily caps for each level of rarity of food items: normal, rare, and epic. There are also mining nodes for refined ore on all world maps, excluding the beginning zone. These mining nodes also have a daily cap. The daily cap allows all players to have an opportunity to collect food materials and refined ore without having to fight other players trying to hoard these resources.

The rarity level of a material affects the number of that material on a map. Normal food items are plentiful. Rare items are fewer in number but still easy to gather. Epic materials spawn 1–2 per map with a respawn timer.

Normal food material nodes can give either normal or rare quality materials. Rare nodes can give rare or epic quality materials. Epic nodes can give epic or legendary quality food materials.

Each zone map also has mushrooms and green grains that are considered food materials, but there is no limit to how much you can farm these two materials. These materials are useful for normal HP replenishment foods and some rare level recipes.

Food System Breakdown

The SxS food system grants permanent stats by eating for the following stats: Attack, HP, Def, and Speed.

Depending on your class and play style, some stats may not be as high of a priority, but all of these stats are helpful for surviving PvP and PvE content.

Recipes can be obtained from commission quests, the map town vendor, and the auction. Only epic, legendary, and mythic recipes can be sold on the auction. Rare recipes can be bought from town vendors. Epic and legendary recipes have only been seen in commission quests at this point in the game.

Each recipe is tied to a region based on its ingredients. Current known regions are Verdantglade, Cinder Ridge, and Aqualis. Loong Haven should also follow this pattern.

Recipes

Legendary: Sunshine Scramble, Roundberry Cake

Epic: Honeyed Roast, Kanstein Burger, Sweet 'n' Sour Meat, Rounbery Wine

Rare: Stewed Meat and Potatoes, Cheerful Greens, Meat & Veggie Pot

Items to Farm

  • Refined Ore
  • Potato — Normal
  • Rounberry — Rare
  • Bird Egg — Epic

Cinder Ridge

Recipes

Legendary: Solar Halo, Curry Feast

Epic: Fruit Glazed Stew, Tangine Pot Stew, Roasted Meat with Chestnuts, Toasted Sweet Nut

Rare: Mushroom and Meat Skewers, Mushroom Soup, Prickly Refesher

Items to Farm

  • Refined Ore
  • Pear — Normal
  • Chestnut — Rare
  • Oasis — Epic

Aqualis

Recipes

Legendary: Lucky Sashimi

Epic: Ocean's Bounty Platter, Fish Tempura

Rare: Ocean Greens, Verdant Sushi, Oden

Items to Farm

  • Refined Ore
  • Leaf — Normal
  • Fish — Rare
  • Octopus — Epic

Special note: Any water location can spawn fish and octopus. Leaf can spawn on land or water. Fish and octopus will not spawn on land areas.
